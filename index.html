SHE MODEL TECH

 

A non-profit dedicated to empowering aspiring women in IT, offering live and on-demand training, mentorship, tech industry networking, and career guidance.

APPLY NOW Learn more

Empower

SHE MODEL TECH actively equips women with the crucial tools and knowledge needed to forge successful careers in technology. By granting access to both live and on-demand IT skills training, the organization ensures participants acquire the technical proficiency vital for excelling in IT roles.

Connect

At the heart of SHE MODEL TECH's mission is the cultivation and support of a vibrant community where women in tech find unity, support, and opportunities for growth. With established mentorship programs led by seasoned professionals and diverse networking opportunities, the organization sustains a nurturing ecosystem.

Thrive

SHE MODEL TECH prioritizes the continuous growth and significant impact of women in the tech sector. The organization supports skill application and career progression through webinars on the latest IT skills and strategies for advancement.

OUR MISSION

  • Offering both live and on-demand training to equip individuals with the essential skills required to start or advance in the tech industry.
  • Connecting women with experienced professionals for guidance in specific tech domains, enhancing their learning and development.
  • Delivering clear insights and strategies for successfully launching and growing a tech career, including understanding industry trends and future directions.
  • Focusing on the mastery of fundamental and advanced tech skills, ensuring participants are up-to-date with the latest technological advancements.
  • Emphasizing the development of practical tech skills that can be immediately applied in real-world scenarios, preparing participants for the demands of the tech industry.

START A CAREER IN TECH

  • Access both live and on-demand IT skills training
  • Connect with mentors experienced in your specific tech domain
  • Network with peers navigating their tech careers
  • Receive guidance and solutions for challenges in your tech journey

GROW YOUR CAREER IN TECH
