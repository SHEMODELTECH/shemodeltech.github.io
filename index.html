SHE MODEL TECH

 

A non-profit dedicated to empowering women in tech through online IT courses, mentorship, networking, and career guidance, aiming to foster an ecosystem for their success in tech.

APPLY NOW Learn more

Empower

SHE MODEL TECH actively equips women with the crucial tools and knowledge needed to forge successful careers in technology. By granting access to online IT courses, the organization ensures participants acquire the technical proficiency vital for excelling in tech roles. This initiative aims to enhance confidence and capability, fully preparing women to exceed industry standards.

Connect

At the heart of SHE MODEL TECH's mission is the cultivation and support of a vibrant community where women in tech find unity, support, and opportunities for growth. With established mentorship programs led by seasoned professionals and diverse networking opportunities, the organization sustains a nurturing ecosystem. This network is a catalyst for the exchange of ideas, collaboration, and the creation of valuable professional relationships, driving personal and career development.

Thrive

SHE MODEL TECH emphasizes the growth and influence of women in tech by organizing an annual tech summit that educates and exposes women to various IT fields, featuring discussions on in-demand skills, leadership, and opportunities, and bringing together thought leaders and professionals to create a knowledge and networking powerhouse, aiming to inspire and empower women to confidently navigate the tech world, achieve leadership roles, and contribute to innovation and growth.

Mission

  • To provide access to online IT courses, equipping women with the essential skills required to start or advance in the tech industry.
  • To connect women with experienced professionals for guidance in specific tech domains, enhancing their learning and development.
  • To facilitate connections with peers who are also navigating their tech careers, fostering a supportive community.
  • To offer tailored advice and solutions to address the unique challenges encountered on the tech career path.

Mentorship

  • Connect with mentors experienced in your specific tech domain
  • Network with peers navigating their tech careers
  • Receive guidance and solutions for challenges in your tech journey
  • Access a wealth of resources, including e-books, tutorials, and online IT courses
  • Navigate your career successfully with our mentors' guided roadmap

Tech Summit

The annual SHE MODEL TECH Summit champions the empowerment of women in technology by providing a platform for education, discussions on vital IT skills and leadership, and unparalleled networking with industry leaders, ultimately aiming to inspire women to excel in tech careers and leadership positions.
